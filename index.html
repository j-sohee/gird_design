The Harvard Film Archive’s cinematheque presents films Friday through Monday year round. Open to the public, all screenings are held in the Archive's 188-seat theater featuring exceptional film and digital projection located in the historic Carpenter Center for the Visual Arts, the only building in North America designed by influential architect Le Corbusier.
